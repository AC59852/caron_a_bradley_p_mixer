Music Box

Instructions:

To use the mixer drag an instrument onto the score. Each instrument has a unique sound and by moving the instrument up or down on the score it will change the pitch. To play the track, hit the play button in the top left. To pause the track hit the pause button to the right of the play button. To change the background, click one of the buttons in the top right.
